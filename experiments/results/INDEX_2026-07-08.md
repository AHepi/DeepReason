## Repairing the 12B's failures with flash + pro
- small_model_repair_prereg.yaml + small_model_repair_report.json —
  H-repair CONFIRMED on all predictions. The 12B's 63 dead-on-arrival
  candidates were FORMAT failures: a single reasoning-off flash pass
  recovered 57/63 (90%) into valid skeletons, 24 (38% of corpus) into
  genuine live survivors, at median claim-fidelity 1.0 (12B reasoning
  preserved verbatim). flash == pro on recovery (57/63 each; pro +4
  survivors) — no reason to pay for pro. Deterministic control only
  16%. Cost 87k DeepSeek tokens, zero new 12B tokens (corpus is the
  committed 12B log).
- Unpredicted: reasoning OFF beat reasoning ON for repair — 57 vs 46
  recovered at 1/3 the tokens (thinking disabled per
  src/deepreason/llm/providers.py). Residual gap: 90% recovered but
  38% survive — syntax repair can't fix a self-defeating forbidden
  case (a 12B generation limit, not format).
- Operating implication (NOT yet applied): keep the small model as
  generator, add a cheap reasoning-off flash JSON/predicate repair
  pass. Script mini/scripts/small_model_repair.py (ReasoningOffEndpoint
  subclass; call.py untouched).
